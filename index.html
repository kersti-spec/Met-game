Met museum artwork year guesser

Welcome to the Met Museum Artwork Year Guesser!

How to Play

  • A random artwork from the Metropolitan Museum of Art will be displayed
  • Look at the artwork and try to determine when it was created
  • Guess whether the artwork was created before or after 1800
  • Click either "Before 1800" or "After 1800" to make your guess
  • See if you're correct and learn more about the artwork!
